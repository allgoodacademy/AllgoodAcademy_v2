The Educational Games Catalog

Welcome to our Free Games Hub! We believe the best way to move from passive learning into pragmatic action is by doing. Dive into our collection of interactive challenges designed to help you build practical skills, master new topics, and achieve real-world results in a fun, engaging way.

Jolene's Lemonade Challenge: Entrepreneur to Empire

Test your entrepreneurial grit by guiding Jolene through real-world business challenges, from managing finances to scaling her product.

Jolene's Lemonade Challenge: Entrepreneur to Empire

Game Description

This interactive scenario game puts you in charge of a startup lemonade business. Make tough decisions on pricing, marketing, supply chain, and resilience to see if you have the strategic mindset needed to turn a small stand into a national empire.

Learning Objectives

  • Apply fundamental principles of business finance and budgeting.
  • Demonstrate market responsiveness and customer feedback integration.
  • Practice strategic decision-making regarding competition and scaling.

Digital Decisions Challenge: Managing Your Online Footprint

Test your digital citizenship and social skills by navigating 30 real-world scenarios. Learn to protect your privacy, combat misinformation, and communicate ethically online.

Digital Decisions Challenge: Managing Your Online Footprint

Game Description

The Digital Decisions Challenge is an interactive training exercise that puts you in control of your online identity. You will face 30 realistic, high-stakes scenarios involving privacy, online communication, misinformation, and ethical dilemmas. Your choices are categorized by effectiveness to help you immediately see the impact of your actions and build a purpose-driven digital brand.

Learning Objectives

  • Demonstrate ethical communication and empathy when resolving online conflicts or offering constructive feedback.
  • Apply critical thinking to identify phishing, misinformation, and unreliable sources.
  • Manage and protect your digital footprint by practicing strong password hygiene and controlling personal data sharing.
  • Uphold digital integrity by respecting intellectual property and responding appropriately to cyberbullying or privacy violations.

Conflict Resolution Challenge: Navigating Disagreement

Master high-stakes communication by using empathy and assertive language to mediate disagreements, set boundaries, and turn everyday friction into positive collaboration in both digital and real-world settings.

Conflict Resolution Challenge: Navigating Disagreement

Game Description

The Conflict Resolution Challenge is an interactive training simulation designed to sharpen your social and emotional intelligence in both personal and professional scenarios. You will practice assertive communication, non-judgmental mediation, and boundary-setting strategies necessary to resolve conflicts, prevent misunderstandings, and build strong, purposeful relationships.

Learning Objectives

  • Master assertive communication strategies, including the use of "I-statements," to express needs without blame.
  • Demonstrate collaborative leadership by effectively mediating group friction and steering teams toward consensus.
  • Develop the ability to set and maintain healthy boundaries in personal and digital interactions (e.g., managing interruptions, loaning items, dealing with constant texts).

Purposeful Communicator Challenge: Master Your Message

Master the art of positive influence and clear communication. Learn to craft messages, give constructive feedback, and lead digital conversations with purpose and empathy.

Purposeful Communicator Challenge: Master Your Message

Game Description

This interactive training focuses on transforming how you express yourself in the digital age. You will learn to shift from reactive posting to proactive, thoughtful communication. Scenarios focus on mastering tone, giving and receiving constructive feedback, leading group discussions, and building a reputation as a reliable and respected voice in any community.

Learning Objectives

  • Craft messages that are clear, empathetic, and constructive in online and real-time environments.
  • Practice active listening and validating others' perspectives to strengthen relationships.
  • Utilize feedback techniques to mentor peers and guide group conversations effectively.
  • Develop a reputation as a reliable and purposeful leader through consistent, positive communication habits.
